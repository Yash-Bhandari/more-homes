MORE HOMES CANADA

Background

  • Housing starts in Canada peaked in the 1970s, even though our population has doubled since then.

  • The rental vacancy rate across the country is at a record low of 1.9% and it’s even lower in the most expensive cities.

  • Low vacancy rates drive large rent hikes.

  • There isn't some massive secret supply of vacant homes.

Our Goals 

  • "Missing Middle" housing projects are often killed by slow, expensive and risky rezoning processes.

  • Affordable housing projects are impacted the most and often face significant pushback at public hearings.

Who We Are

More Homes Canada is a coalition of local housing advocacy groups. Together, we are fighting to end Canada’s housing shortage.
